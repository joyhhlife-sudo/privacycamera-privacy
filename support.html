FAQ / 常见问题

1. Are my photos or videos uploaded?
1. 我的照片或视频会被上传吗？

No. Privacy processing is designed to run on device. We do not upload your photos, videos, or face data.

不会。本 App 的隐私处理设计为在设备本地完成。我们不会上传你的照片、视频或人脸数据。

2. Why does the app request photo library access?
2. 为什么 App 请求照片图库权限？

The app uses photo library access to import photos or videos, show the latest library thumbnail, and save protected results.

App 使用照片图库权限来导入照片或视频、显示最近照片预览，并保存已保护结果。

3. Will the app modify my original photos or videos?
3. App 会修改我的原始照片或视频吗？

No. The app does not delete or overwrite your original media. Protected results are saved as new files.

不会。App 不会删除或覆盖你的原始媒体。已保护结果会作为新文件保存。

4. Why are some faces or eye regions not masked?
4. 为什么有些人脸或人眼没有被遮挡？

Automatic detection may be affected by lighting, angle, motion, distance, occlusion, and device performance. Please review saved results before sharing.

自动检测可能受到光线、角度、运动、距离、遮挡和设备性能影响。公开分享前请检查保存结果。

5. Is the app free?
5. App 是免费的吗？

Version 1.0 is planned as a free app. Future versions may add optional paid Pro features, but the basic free features in version 1.0 are intended to remain available.

1.0 版本计划免费提供。未来版本可能加入可选的 Pro 付费功能，但 1.0 的基础免费功能计划继续保留。
