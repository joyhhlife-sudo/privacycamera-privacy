Privacy Policy
隐私政策

Effective Date: May 31, 2026
生效日期：2026年5月31日

1. Overview / 概述

Privacy Camera is a camera and media protection app designed to help users protect privacy in photos and videos. The app can apply visual protection effects such as blur, mosaic, or block masks to faces, eye regions, or other selected privacy-sensitive areas before saving protected photos or videos.

隐私相机是一款用于帮助用户保护照片和视频隐私的相机与媒体保护 App。本 App 可以在人脸、人眼区域或其他用户选择的隐私区域添加模糊、马赛克、色块遮挡等视觉保护效果，并保存已保护的照片或视频。

2. Information We Do Not Collect / 我们不收集的信息

We do not collect, upload, store on our servers, sell, or share your photos, videos, face data, eye data, biometric identifiers, or personal identity information.

We do not require account registration. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, advertising identifier, or payment credentials.

我们不会收集、上传、在我们的服务器上存储、出售或分享你的照片、视频、人脸数据、人眼数据、生物识别信息或个人身份信息。

本 App 不需要账号注册。我们不会收集你的姓名、邮箱、手机号、通讯录、精确位置、广告标识符或支付凭证。

3. Photo and Video Processing / 照片与视频处理

Privacy Camera is designed to process photos and videos on your device. Privacy masks, blur effects, mosaic effects, watermark rendering, photo export, and video export are designed to be performed locally on your device.

Your photos and videos are not uploaded to our servers for processing.

隐私相机设计为在你的设备本地处理照片和视频。隐私遮挡、模糊效果、马赛克效果、水印渲染、照片导出和视频导出设计为在设备本地完成。

你的照片和视频不会被上传到我们的服务器进行处理。

4. Camera Permission / 相机权限

The app requests camera access so you can capture photos and videos and apply privacy protection during capture.

本 App 请求相机权限，用于拍摄照片和视频，并在拍摄过程中进行隐私保护处理。

5. Photo Library Permission / 照片图库权限

The app may request photo library access to import photos or videos, show the latest library thumbnail, and save protected results.

The app does not delete or overwrite your original photos or videos. Protected results are saved as new media files.

本 App 可能请求照片图库权限，用于导入照片或视频、显示最近照片预览，并保存已保护结果。

本 App 不会删除或覆盖你的原始照片或视频。已保护结果会作为新的媒体文件保存。

6. Microphone Permission / 麦克风权限

If video recording with audio is enabled, the app may request microphone access to record audio with your videos.

如果启用带声音的视频录制，本 App 可能请求麦克风权限，用于录制视频声音。

7. Protected Output and Detection Limits / 已保护输出与检测限制

The app is designed to save protected photos and videos. Depending on your settings, protected output may include blur, mosaic, block masks, or watermark information.

Please review saved results before sharing or publishing them. Automatic detection and masking may be incomplete or inaccurate due to lighting, angle, distance, occlusion, motion blur, small faces, overlapping people, device performance, or other factors.

The app does not guarantee complete or error-free privacy protection in every situation.

本 App 的目标是保存已保护的照片和视频。根据你的设置，已保护结果可能包含模糊、马赛克、色块遮挡或水印信息。

公开分享或发布前，请自行检查保存结果。自动检测和遮挡可能受到光线、角度、距离、遮挡、运动模糊、人脸过小、多人重叠、设备性能等因素影响，导致遮挡不完整或不准确。

本 App 不保证在所有场景下都能提供完整或无错误的隐私保护。

8. Free Allowance and Local Usage Records / 免费额度与本地使用记录

The app may provide a free version with limited usage allowances. Free allowances may include limited saves for face-masked photos, eye-masked photos, face-masked videos, eye-masked videos, and imported photo processing. These allowances are calculated based on the device’s local calendar date and refresh every 2 local calendar days.

To determine free allowance and Pro access, the app may store purchase status, free usage counts, and allowance refresh periods locally on your device. This information is used to determine feature availability on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

本 App 可能提供带有限使用额度的免费版本。免费额度可能包括人脸遮挡照片、人眼遮挡照片、人脸遮挡视频、人眼遮挡视频和导入照片处理的有限保存次数。免费额度按设备本地日期计算，每 2 个本地自然日刷新一次。

为了判断免费额度和 Pro 权益，App 可能会在设备本地保存购买状态、免费使用次数和额度刷新周期。这些信息用于在本机判断功能是否可用，不会上传到我们的服务器。

9. In-App Purchases / App 内购买

The app may offer an optional one-time Pro unlock as a non-consumable in-app purchase. The Pro unlock may provide unlimited saves, imported photo processing, videos up to 1 hour, mosaic masking, more colors, and more masking styles.

In-app purchases are processed by Apple App Store using Apple StoreKit. Purchases, payments, refunds, and purchase records are handled by Apple. We do not directly collect or process your bank card numbers, payment passwords, or other payment credentials.

本 App 可能提供可选的一次性 Pro 解锁，作为非消耗型 App 内购买项目。Pro 解锁可能提供不限次数保存、导入照片处理不限次数、视频最长支持 1 小时、马赛克遮挡、更多颜色和更多遮挡样式。

App 内购买通过 Apple StoreKit 由 Apple App Store 处理。购买、付款、退款和购买记录由 Apple 处理。我们不会直接收集或处理你的银行卡号、支付密码或其他支付凭证。
