Privacy Policy
隐私政策

Effective Date: May 27, 2026
生效日期：2026年5月27日

1. Overview / 概述

Privacy Camera is a camera app designed to help users protect privacy in photos and videos. The app can apply visual protection effects such as blur, mosaic, or masks to faces, eye regions, or other selected privacy-sensitive areas before saving protected photos or videos.

隐私相机是一款用于帮助用户保护照片和视频隐私的相机 App。本 App 可以在人脸、人眼区域或其他用户选择的隐私区域添加模糊、马赛克、遮挡等视觉保护效果，并保存已保护的照片或视频。

2. Information We Do Not Collect / 我们不收集的信息

We do not collect, upload, store, sell, or share your photos, videos, face data, biometric identifiers, or personal identity information.

We do not require account registration. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, advertising identifier, or payment information.

我们不会收集、上传、存储、出售或分享你的照片、视频、人脸数据、生物识别信息或个人身份信息。

本 App 不需要账号注册。我们不会收集你的姓名、邮箱、手机号、通讯录、精确位置、广告标识符或支付信息。

3. Photo and Video Processing / 照片与视频处理

Privacy Camera processes photos and videos on your device. Privacy masks, blur, mosaic effects, watermark rendering, photo export, and video export are designed to be performed locally on your device.

Your photos and videos are not uploaded to our servers for processing.

隐私相机在你的设备本地处理照片和视频。隐私遮挡、模糊、马赛克、水印渲染、照片导出和视频导出设计为在设备本地完成。

你的照片和视频不会被上传到我们的服务器进行处理。

4. Camera Permission / 相机权限

The app requests camera access so you can capture photos and videos and apply privacy protection during capture.

本 App 请求相机权限，用于拍摄照片和视频，并在拍摄过程中进行隐私保护处理。

5. Photo Library Permission / 照片图库权限

The app may request photo library access to import photos or videos, show the latest library thumbnail, and save protected results.

The app does not delete or overwrite your original photos or videos. Protected results are saved as new media files.

本 App 可能请求照片图库权限，用于导入照片或视频、显示最近照片预览，并保存已保护结果。

本 App 不会删除或覆盖你的原始照片或视频。已保护结果会作为新的媒体文件保存。

6. Microphone Permission / 麦克风权限

If video recording with audio is enabled, the app may request microphone access to record audio with your videos.

如果启用带声音的视频录制，本 App 可能请求麦克风权限，用于录制视频声音。

7. Protected Output / 已保护输出

The app is designed to save protected photos and videos. Depending on your settings, protected output may include blur, mosaic, masks, or watermark information.

Please review saved results before sharing. Automatic detection and masking may vary depending on lighting, angle, occlusion, distance, motion, and device performance.

本 App 的目标是保存已保护的照片和视频。根据你的设置，已保护结果可能包含模糊、马赛克、遮挡或水印信息。

公开分享前请检查保存结果。自动检测和遮挡效果可能受到光线、角度、遮挡、距离、运动和设备性能影响。

8. Third-Party Services / 第三方服务

The current version does not use third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, account systems, or cloud processing services.

If third-party services are added in the future, this Privacy Policy and the App Store privacy information will be updated accordingly.

当前版本不使用第三方广告 SDK、统计分析 SDK、账号系统或云端处理服务。

如果未来加入第三方服务，我们会更新本隐私政策和 App Store 隐私信息。

9. Tracking and Advertising / 追踪与广告

We do not track you across apps or websites. We do not use your data for advertising tracking.

我们不会跨 App 或网站追踪你。我们不会将你的数据用于广告追踪。

We may update this Privacy Policy when app features change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

当 App 功能发生变化时，我们可能更新本隐私政策。更新后的版本会发布在本页面，并注明新的生效日期。
